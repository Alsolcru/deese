Our History

It all began with a university thesis project. Driven by passion for technology, we developed an electronic device capable of communicating with a vehicle's engine computer. This small but powerful device could read key parameters like engine temperature, fuel level and other important data, combine it with GPS positioning information, and send everything to a server for real-time visualization. This was the beginning of what is now DeEse.

What started as an academic project soon became something bigger. We decided to test it in the real world, and thus DeEse was officially born. From our early years, we provided telemetry services using European tracking units while continuing to develop and improve our own solutions. Each step brought us closer to our goal: offering cutting-edge technology for fleet management.

Our dedication and expertise didn't go unnoticed. A leading Dutch telemetry development company contacted us to be their technical support in Latin America. Soon after, a renowned Colombian company also trusted us for the same. These collaborations not only allowed us to grow professionally but also provided us with enriching experiences and lasting friendships.
